NOTE: Master Mix Below refers to Apex RED Taq DNA Polymerase Master Mix, 2.0X,  Cat No. 42-138, Genesee Scientific, San Diego CA

Primer sequences:
Primer A:
	Fwd: CTGATAGTATGATTGGTAAT
	Rev: ATAAGTTCTCCAACTATCTT

Primer B:
	Fwd: CCAGTATCTCCATAAGCATC
	Rev: GTGAGGGCGGAATAGCTA

Primer C:
	Fwd: GCAACAGGAGTAGTAAAATCTC
	Rev: GCTCCTGTTAATCCTGATGTTA



To prepare the DNA template, we take raw sewage influent (the stuff coming into the sewage plant), centrifuge it briefly to remove the solids, and pass it through a 0.2 or 0.22 µm filter. We just use that in the reaction with no further treatment.

PCR mixture:

Reagent	Volume
DNA template		7.0 µl
2x Master Mix		25.0 µl
Forward primer (10µm)	2.0 µl
Reverse primer (10µm)	2.0 µl
DNAse free water 	14.0 µl
Total volume		50.0 µl



Amplification protocols:

Primer A:
Denture 95¼C for 3 minutes.

Then 30 cycles of:
	Denature 95¼C for 45 seconds
	Annealing 42.6¼C for 30 seconds
	Extension 68¼C for 90 seconds

Final extension	68¼C for 5 minutes


Primers B & C:
Denture 95¼C for 3 minutes.

Then 30 cycles of:
	Denature 95¼C for 45 seconds
	Annealing 50¼C for 30 seconds
	Extension 68¼C for 90 seconds

Final extension	68¼C for 5 minutes
